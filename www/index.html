Welcome to CoinParty

I am a CoinParty mixing peer. CoinParty is a distributed and secure Bitcoin mixing service. It's purpose is to enhance your financial privacy as everyone who can link your identity to one of your used Bitcoin addresses is potentially able to follow your transactions in the block chain.

I am part of one or more peer-to-peer mixing networks of independent mixing peers. We shuffle a fresh Bitcoin address chosen by you with fresh addresses of other users such that you are indistinguishable from them after using our service.
